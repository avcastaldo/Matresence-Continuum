Why Matrescence Continuum?

My name is Antonella Castaldo. I became a mother on my own terms — a single mom by choice — stepping into matrescence with so much love, and no family nearby to lean on. I was determined, hopeful, and figuring it out as I went.

I spent many late nights reading, researching, searching for the right support — lactation consultants, doulas, postpartum care, pelvic floor therapy. Not because I couldn't handle it, but because I wanted someone to help me find my way.

You don't have to do that alone.

Matrescence Continuum is the guide I wish I had. One person who knows your story, connects you to the right practitioners, and holds the thread through every stage. You were never meant to do this alone — and now, you don't have to.

— Antonella Castaldo

Why Matrescence Continuum?

Matrescence is a journey. Yours deserves a guide.

The transition into motherhood is one of the most profound transformations a woman can experience — yet most women navigate it alone, piecing together care from multiple different practitioners, multiple different intake forms, and no one holding the thread.

We believe you deserve better. A single guide who knows your story. A curated network of practitioners you can trust. A plan that evolves with you — from trying to conceive through every trimester, every fourth-trimester challenge, and beyond.

We don't replace your doctor or your doula. We're the thread that weaves them together — so nothing falls through the cracks.
